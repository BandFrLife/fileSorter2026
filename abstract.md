    College students often find that it can be difficult to organize, especially at the start of each semester. However, in the chaos of unpredicted challenges at the start of classes, students may end up falling behind quickly. For this reason, it is important that students prepare with what is in their control and let it carry them throughout the program. This project aims to aid students in file finding, sorting, and organization using a simple setup that will carry from term to term. A student can prepare not only by semesters but efficiently refer to previous semesters and prepare for future ones. Compared to a normal process of using a filesystem, a GUI will allow users to quickly process their files, use a tagging system to help both storing files in the appropriate place, and searching tags to find them quickly. This will help students create a quick setup of semester, class, and homework directories with little input from the user. The consistency will streamline a major portion of a student's work flow via this organization tool and result in a shift in stress away from the smaller things.
